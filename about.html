ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE


Ptarmigan is a custom millwork and renovation company in Edmonton. We support locally sourced and sustainable materials. We proudly believe in the cultivation of elbow grease, grit, and love through an honest days work.

WHAT WE DO


We specialize in custom high quality hardwood furniture and residential space solutions. Everything we make is designed and made in Edmonton from sustainably sourced materials and put together using timeless hand-craft techniques.
