HEY THERE!

I am currently a 3rd year computer science student majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Digital Design. I started photography back in high school as a hobby and fell in love with capturing moments with a simple camera. Since then, I had the opportunity to capture many special events of friends and loved ones such as birthdays, engagement shots, weddings, and family pictures.

I have also explored videography as another hobby and started working with a gimbal. If I'm not completing school work or any of the hobbies listed above, I am usually binge-watching netflix.
